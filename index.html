About Us

Built for the Chaos of Live Music

Panic Booking was born out of the DIY scene — venues that needed bands, bands that needed shows, and nobody to connect them on short notice. So we fixed that.

Led by Kat Rodriguez, our agency works across the full spectrum of the live music industry, from sweaty basement shows to legitimate club stages. We know both worlds, and we move fast in both.

We don't believe in over-promising. We believe in answering the phone, knowing our roster cold, and making deals that work for everyone involved. That's it.

■ Fast communication — always
■ Transparent deals, no hidden fees
■ Deep roots in the punk / DIY scene
■ The show always goes on
